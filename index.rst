Welcome to the FDSN's documentation
===================================

The `International Federation of Digital Seismograph Networks
<https://www.fdsn.org/>`_ (FDSN) produces and maintains standards
specifications and other documentation for seismology.

Publications, including some legacy documentation can be found
at https://www.fdsn.org/publications/.
